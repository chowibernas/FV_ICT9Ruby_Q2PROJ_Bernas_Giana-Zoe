Beef Salpicao




...


Ingredients...
  • Rib-eye steak cut into cubes
  • Garlic cloves sliced thinly
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Garlic
  • White Onion
  • Cold butter
  • Low sodium soy sauce
  • Brown sugar or Oyster Sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Avocado oil for frying
Instructions...
  • Prepare crunchy garlic and shallots. Take away and place aside.

  • Brown the beef for 2 minutes on each side.

  • Take out the beef and include the sauce ingredients. Add the cold butter at the end to achieve a smooth sauce.

  • Put the beef back in, mix, and serve! Enjoy!

  • At medium heat, add 3 tablespoons of avocado oil. After the oil becomes hot, gently add the garlic that has been sliced thinly. Saut√© garlic and shallots until they reach a golden brown color and become wonderfully crispy.

  • Increase the temperature to medium-high. Incorporate the bite-sized ribeye pieces and grill them until flawlessly cooked.

  • Add the chicken broth or water, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, or oyster sauce. Place the cold butter in the pan, letting it melt and cover the beef.

  • Mix the beef until thoroughly blended. Serve with a pinch of rice.
