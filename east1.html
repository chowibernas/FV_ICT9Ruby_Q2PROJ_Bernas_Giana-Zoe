Shrimp Fried Rice




...


Ingredients...
  • 8 ounces small raw shrimp, shelled and deveined
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper/li>
  • 1/2 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons peanut oil, canola oil, or rice bran oil
  • 3 large eggs, beate
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • 4 cups leftover rice, grains separated well
  • 3/4 cup frozen peas and carrots, defrosted
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dark toasted sesame oil
Instructions...
  • Sprinkle shrimp with salt,pepper and cornstarch and throw on top.Set aside to sit for ten minutes at room temperature.

  • Heat a large skillet or oven.When the oven is very hot, place a tablespoon of cooking oil on the oven, swirl, and add shrimp to the hot oven.

  • Cook for 30 seconds in the pan,without moving. Cook the eggs until they are largely cooked, then flip the eggs, place them in a bowl, and then take them out of the pan and cook until they are slightly cooked.

  • Cook the beaten eggs until slightly runny

  • Add soy sauce, carrots,beans,shrimp,eggsand sesameoil, and serve.
