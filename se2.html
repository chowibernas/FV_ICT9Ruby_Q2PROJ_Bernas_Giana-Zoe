Chicken Lumpia




...


Ingredients...
  • Ground Chicken
  • Spring Onion
  • Celery Leaves
  • Carrot
  • White Onion
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Cornstarch
  • Spring Roll Wrapper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
Instructions...
  • Prepare crunchy garlic and shallots. Take out and place aside. Blend the ground chicken with the vegetables, soy sauce, and the other ingredients in a food processor..

  • Put the meat filling in the middle of the wrapper, then roll it up from the bottom to the center.

  • Add vegetable oil to a deep frying pan. When the oil is hot, place each lumpia in and fry them on both sides until they become golden brown. On medium heat, add 3 tablespoons of avocado oil. After the oil is heated, gently introduce the thinly sliced garlic. Saut√© garlic and shallots until they reach a golden brown color and become perfectly crispy.
  • Serve with a pinch of rice
