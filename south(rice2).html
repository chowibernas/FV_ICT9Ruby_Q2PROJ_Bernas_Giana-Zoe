Tomato Rice (Thakkali Sadam)




...


Ingredients...
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 1.5 cups long grain white rice
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 15oz. can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
Instructions...
  • Dice the onion and mince the garlic. Put the cooking oil, garlic, and onion in a pot and cook over medium heat until the onions are transparent and tender.

  • Add the rice, cumin, and chili powder to the pot. To toast the rice and spices, keep stirring and cooking for a few more minutes.

  • Add the broth, tomato paste, and diced tomatoes (with their juices) to the pot. Stir.

  • Cover the saucepan, increase the heat to high, and let the soup to boil. After the broth reaches a boiling point, reduce the heat to a simmer.

  • Saut√© the onion for around ten minutes, or until it is soft and has a deep golden brown hue.Once you've rested, serve.

  • Allow the rice to simmer softly for 20 minutes without removing the lid or stirring. After 20 minutes, take the pot off the heat and allow the rice to sit for five minutes.

  • Incorporate water and salt, then heat until boiling. Cover and lower the heat to low. Cook on low heat until all the water is gone, approximately 15 minutes.

  • After rest, serve.
