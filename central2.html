  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon water
  • 2 onions, peeled
  • ½ pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 (8 ounce) container plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
Instructions...
  • Make dough: Combine flour and salt in a large bowl. Add eggs and water, then mix well with your hands. Add more water, if needed, to form a soft dough.Cover then set aside.

  • Cut the onions and place them in a sieveon a bowl. In a medium bowl, mix the dissolved onion fried beef, salt, and pepper and mix well with a spoon until they are broken. Set it aside.

  • Lightflourthework surface and a large plate. Divide the dough into half. Working on a piece at a time, roll the dough on a floured surface into a rectangle as thin as possible.

  • Place about 2 teaspoons in the center of each square. Gather the edges of the dough and pres them at the top to form a package. Transfer the manti to the prepared plate.

  • Mix the yogurt and garlic in a bowl then put it aside.

  • Pour yogurt sauce on the manti and sprinkle with pepper oil then serve.
