Plov
  • 1 1/2 lbs Beef chuck, beef sirloin or good quality beef stew meat
  • 1/3 cup canola oil, or extra light olive oil
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp salt for the meat and veggies + 1 1/2 tsp salt for the rice
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 1 3/4 cups hot water for braising meat
  • 3 cups long grain rice
  • 4 cups hot water when cooking rice
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
Instructions...
  • Trimbeef of excess fat andsinews (akathe chewy stuff),pat the meatdry witha papertowel and chop into1/2″ to3/4″ pieces.

  • Preheat a large souppot/dutch oven. Stir 1/3 cup of granola oil. Once the oil is hot, addchopped meat andsaute uncovered 7 min over high heat until meatit browned.

  • Reduce heat to medium andAdd chopped onion, stirringoften until onion issoftened (5 minutes).Stir in sliced carrots,1 tsp salt, 1/2tsp pepper, 1tsp paprika, 1tsp cumin, 3-4 bayleaves andcontinue to cookover medium heat 5minutes until carrots aresoftened.

  • Add 1 3/4 cups hot water, cover & simmer over medium/low heat 45 min or until meat is tender.

  • Meanwhile, rinse rice until water runs clear, then drain and set aside

  • Spread rice over the meat and add 4 cups hot water. Sprinkle the rice with 1 1/2tsp saltand DO NOT STIR, bring toa boil thenreduce heat to low andLet cook uncovered until most of the water is absorbed for 10minutes.

  • Put your head of garlic, cut side down into the center of the rice and sprinkle the top of the rice with 1 tsp ground coriander

  • Poke 7-10 holes through the rice to allow steam to escape to the surface, reduce the heat to low, thencover and cook until riceis cooked through. Once cooked, serve.
