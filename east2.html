SOUTH ASIAN RECIPES...




Gyudon




...


Ingredients...
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Dashi Stock
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Cooked Rice
Instructions...
  • Add Dashi stock, soy sauce, evaporated cane sugar and chopped onions to the pan and heat to boil the mixture. Reducing the heat to a gentle simmer and cooking onions until transparent.This takes about three minutes.

  • Step beef and cook with onion and sauce until it reaches its peak.

  • Serve cooked rice in a large bowl and then use a spoon to serve beef and sauce on rice. Garnish your gyudon with your favorite toppings and serve.
